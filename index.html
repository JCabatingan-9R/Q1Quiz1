The Emoji Movie


Inside a smartphone lies Textopolis: a home for emojis.
Usually emojis only have one expression, but Gene seems to have more than one, therefore becoming a threat
to the other emojis. He travels through other apps with the help of his friends, Hi-5 and Jailbreak,
in order to escape the leader of the emoji, Smiler.


  • Gene (T.J. Miller)
  • Hi-5 (James Corden)
  • Jailbreak (Anna Faris)
  • Smiler (Maya Rudolph)
  • Poop Daddy (Patrick Stewart)
